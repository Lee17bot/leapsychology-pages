Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lea?

Lea is an AI-powered psychology companion app that provides supportive conversations about mental wellness. You can talk about anxiety, stress, relationships, self-esteem, sleep, and many other topics. Lea is available 24/7 in English and Romanian.

Is Lea a therapist?

No. Lea is an AI companion and is NOT a replacement for professional therapy or medical advice. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed professional or use the crisis resources listed below.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your conversations are stored locally on your device. We do not store your conversations on any server. When you send a message, it is processed by our AI service to generate a response, but it is not permanently stored. You can export or delete all your data at any time from Settings.

How do I sign in?

Lea uses Sign in with Apple for a secure, private authentication experience. You can choose to share or hide your email address. Apple may provide a private relay email to protect your real address.

What is Lea Premium?

The free version of Lea includes 20 messages. Lea Premium unlocks unlimited conversations. It is a monthly auto-renewable subscription with a 7-day free trial. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID Account Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select Lea Premium → Cancel Subscription. You will continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID and go to Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored automatically.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Your Data → Delete All Data. This permanently deletes all conversations and messages from your device. This action cannot be undone.

How do I sign out?

Go to Settings → Account → Sign Out. This clears your authentication credentials. Your conversation data remains on the device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

What languages does Lea support?

Lea currently supports English and Romanian. You can change your language during onboarding or in Settings → Profile → Language.

Crisis Resources

If you are in crisis, please reach out for help:

  • US - Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Romania - Telefonul Sufletului: 0800 801 200
  • International - Find a Helpline: findahelpline.com
  • Emergency: Call 112 (EU) or 911 (US)

Remember: Lea is an AI companion designed to support your wellness journey, but it is not a substitute for professional help. If you or someone you know is in danger, please call emergency services immediately.
