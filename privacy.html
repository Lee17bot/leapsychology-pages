Privacy Policy

Lea - AI Psychology Companion

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Lea ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI psychology companion iOS application developed by Liviu Daniel Pop. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Lea app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Sign in with Apple Data: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique user identifier, and optionally your name and email address (only if you choose to share them). Apple may provide a private relay email address instead of your actual email.
  • Conversation Data: All conversations with Lea are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your conversations on our servers.
  • Preferences: Your app preferences (name, language, selected topics) are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Data: If you subscribe to Lea Premium, Apple handles all payment processing. We do not collect or store any payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: Your Apple ID identifier is used solely to authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • AI Conversations: When you send a message, it is transmitted to our AI service (via a secure Cloudflare proxy) to generate a response. Messages are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Personalization: Your name and selected topics are used locally to personalize your experience within the app.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All your conversations, preferences, and personal data are stored locally on your iOS device. We do not maintain a cloud database of user data.
  • Keychain: Your authentication credentials are securely stored in the iOS Keychain, Apple's encrypted credential storage system.
  • Encryption: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • No Tracking: We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not track your behavior or create user profiles.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The only external communication is:

  • AI Service: Your messages are sent to our AI service provider (Anthropic Claude, via Cloudflare proxy) to generate responses. These messages are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored by the service.
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple authentication and subscription management are handled by Apple per their privacy policy.

6. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export all your conversations in JSON format from Settings.
  • Delete: You can delete all your conversations and data from Settings at any time.
  • Sign Out: You can sign out at any time, which clears your authentication credentials from the device.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

Lea is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
